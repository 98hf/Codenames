CODENAMES (webversion) by Ismael Tosun on March 26, 2026
inspired by the CODENAMES board game by Vlaada Chvátil

How to play :
- Atleast 4 players
- 2 teams, red and blue
- One person from both teams is a Spymaster, the rest are Operatives
- Operatives need to guess the right cards for their team, first one to guess all on their color wins
- Black card eliminates the team that guesses it
- Blank (Grey) cards do nothing
- Red and blue cards are for the red and blue teams
- Spymaster must get their operatives to guess the right cards by coming up
  with a code word or short phrase (code word) that categorizes the desired words, and a number of guesses (1-5)
- Operatives can only guess for their team on their turn
- Red goes first as it has 9 cards instead of 8
- Spymasters will be able to see the color of all cards seperately

Interface :
- top left displays the score for both teams and the remaining guesses for the Operatives
- cards in the middle are the cards to be guessed. They start beige and reveal the true color once guessed
- Spymasters control the toggle button to switch to their grid where they can see the color of the cards
- The button on the top right is where they can set the code word and number of guesses
- Save button will save the settings
- Cancel button will cancel
